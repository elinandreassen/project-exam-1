    Earth Orbit

    SpaceX works towards being able to let all of us experience Earth from space. The spacecraft "Dragon" fully orbits the Earth every 90 minutes and this makes it possible to customize flying paths. Imagine being able to travel from London to New York in 30 minutes! More detailed information about the Earth Orbit mission can be found on the official SpaceX website by clicking the button below.

    The Moon

    As the Moon is our closest neighbour and is habitable, it gives us huge opportunities to gain experience and explore for missions to Mars and beyond. Did you know that only 24 humans have been to the Moon in history, and that no one has been back since 1972? Well, it's about time! Read more on this mission on the official SpaceX website.

    Mars & Beyond

    Mars is a mysterious planet. A day on Mars is 24hrs and 37mins, the atmosphere is primarily CO2 and we can grow plants in a compressed atmosphere. As it's a bit further from the sun it's colder there, but habitable! SpaceX has this mission to make life multiplanetary and wants to build a reusable transportation system by using Mars' natural H2O and CO2 to refuel on Mars. Exciting, right?! Read all about the mission on SpaceX official website and be amazed!
